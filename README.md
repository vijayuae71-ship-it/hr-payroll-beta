# HR & Payroll Manager — Beta

A complete HR & Payroll Management SaaS platform built for organizations with 10-100 employees.

## 🚀 Live Demo

Deploy this on Netlify and share the link with testers.

## Features

- 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- 👥 Employee & Department Management
- 💰 Payroll Processing
- 📅 Attendance & Leave Management
- 🎓 Training & Development
- 🏆 Performance Appraisals
- 🔍 Talent Acquisition
- 📈 Reports
- 💳 Billing & Subscription Management
- ⚙️ Company Settings
- 🌐 Multi-currency (AED, USD, INR)
- 🏢 Multi-tenant with self-service onboarding
